# Vertex — launch

The video is not a demo and it is not a defence. It's a thesis with an ask.

**Honesty is a constraint on what you say. It is not a thing to say.** Don't advertise integrity —
just be accurate, quietly, and let the precision carry it. Every sentence whose *job* is to make
you trustworthy presupposes an accusation nobody made. Cut those. Keep the sentences that describe
the thing.

## The headline

**Focus is a stat. Train it.**

Lead with it. It's already the language of the audience — aim is a stat, APM is a stat, stats get
trained. `The mind, unbottlenecked` is the horizon line, not the opener; it lands at the end.

## Cut these on sight

Every one of these was in an earlier draft. They all read as guilt.

- ~~"the gap between what neurotech promises and what it delivers is a graveyard"~~ — makes them
  picture the graveyard, with you in it.
- ~~"every spec is defensible or deleted"~~ — great internal rule. Terrible sentence. It answers a
  charge nobody filed.
- ~~"every demo is a real brain"~~ — implies someone suggested it wasn't.
- ~~"the proof is on the site — go break it"~~ — presumes they wanted to.
- ~~"you'll call a fake signal in milliseconds"~~ — flattering, but it summons the fake signal.
  The real reason to start with gamers has no defence in it: they already train everything else.

The manifesto is where this lives. It's a document people opt into. A launch video is not.

## Still true, still unsayable

Not because they're embarrassing — because they're false, and the accuracy is free.

- ~~8 channels~~ — v4 is one single-ended channel.
- ~~250 Hz / ADS1299~~ — that's v5, never flashed on real hardware. v4 is an ADS1220.
- ~~"Berger validated"~~ — not until there's a capture.
- ~~"Discord cost you 23 minutes"~~ — causation from a confound; the number was invented.
- ~~Flow Shield muting Slack~~ — a sandboxed Mac app can't mute other apps or pause tabs.
- ~~"reads your mind"~~ — rung one is one number. Say one number. Overclaiming the *vision* is the
  only thing at this altitude that can still kill you.
